Welcome to TIS Kolkata

The inception of The International School is a noteworthy event, and The International School Kolkata is no exception. TIS Kolkata is driven by the vision of instilling the right moral values in students, fostering their growth into responsible citizens of the future. As a CBSE-affiliated institution, TIS Kolkata is committed to upholding and elevating the highest standards of excellence, preparing successive generations for the challenges of the 21st century.

The school provides a nurturing and innovative environment, aimed at molding students into amiable, confident, and productive members of the community. TIS Kolkata places a healthy emphasis on both curricular and co-curricular programs, seeking to groom students with a focus on developing strength of character, a mindset of excellence, a commitment to teamwork, and leadership potential. This holistic approach aims to shape students into responsible and gracious citizens of the world.

Principal's Message
Ms. Natalya Stepanovna Principal
TIS Kolkata has embarked on a well-defined vision encapsulated by SACH, signifying our belief in Scientific inquiry of the mind, Agility of both mind and body, Creating, nurturing, and preserving nature, and fostering Humility, Unity, and brotherhood.

Our vision, SACH, comprehensively addresses vital aspects for the holistic development of students. In this era of digitization, our focus is on delivering activity-oriented learning, empowering our students, digital natives, to utilize both manual dexterity and mental agility, thus nurturing them into capable global citizens. We accord equal importance to instilling the crucial virtue of humility, recognizing it as a cornerstone of a well-balanced and cultured individual. I trust that our vision of SACH will equip our students to navigate and adeptly handle the challenges presented by the daily rigors of life.
